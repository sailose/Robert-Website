What We Offer

At Fast Fire Defense, we offer a range of training programs designed to meet the needs of both novice and experienced firearm owners. Our courses include:

  • Concealed Carry Weapon (CCW) Permit Training (Maryland)
  • Handgun Qualification License (HQL) Certification
  • DC Concealed Carry Permit Training
  • Firearms Safety and Prevention
  • Firearms Law and Regulations
  • Basic Handgun Skills
  • Advanced Handgun Skills
  • 1 on 1 Training
  • NRA Pistol Qualification, Rifle Qualification, and Concealed Carry Permit
  • Advanced Defensive Firearm Tactics
  • Personal Safety and Self-Defense Strategies

Visit our contact page to schedule a session and learn more about our services.

We are dedicated to providing the highest quality training, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for responsible firearm ownership and personal safety.
