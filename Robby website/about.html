Who We Are

At Fast Fire Defense, we’re a Maryland-based team dedicated to promoting responsible firearm ownership and personal safety. Our expert instructors provide top-tier training for Concealed Carry Weapon (CCW) permits, Handgun Qualification Licenses (HQL), and advanced firearm skills.

With a focus on practical, real-world applications, we combine firearms expertise, self-defense strategies, and legal knowledge to deliver courses for all skill levels.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Maryland residents with comprehensive firearm training, fostering a community of responsible, law-abiding gun owners. We offer education in CCW, HQL, and defensive tactics.

We strive to build confidence, competence, and situational awareness, equipping students with the skills and mindset for safe firearm use in real-world scenarios.
